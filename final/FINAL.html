About Lizzy.


I am a 22 year old student at the University of Miami. I am a senior majoring in Communications and double minoring in Advertising and Public Relations. My major and minors have helped me acquire the necessary skills to develop and expand my knowledge of visual and graphic design. I have found my passion and continue to learn and grow everyday as a young designer!
Other tidbits about me: I am a middle child; I have two awesome brothers! I am extremely passionate about travel and I am a real adventure seeker! I have been to 12 countries and plan on continuing my travels for as long as I possibility can. I am an extraverted personality type and love nothing more then being with the people I love (friedns and family). I am also a bit ADHD. I cannot sit still and neither can my mind; my creative juices are always flowing. An active life is a healthy life, and I think it is important to do as much as you can while you have the chance.
